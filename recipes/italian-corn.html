Irresistible Italian Corn


Description

Italian Corn comes with goodness of corn and aromatic Italian seasoning. A great dish to enjoy with your family and friends.

This food is rich in fiber, minarals and protenes and suitable for children as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of Butter
  • 2 1/2 cups of whole kernel sweet corn
  • 1 tablespoon of Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 1 pinch ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over high heat
  2. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely melted
  3. Add corn into the butter and stri well
  4. Season corn with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Cook seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes
  6. Transfer the coocked corn in to a bowl and enjoy

Mac and cheese


Description

Mac n cheese is a delicious meal that can be easily prepared with few ingrediants in a little time. This dish is most appropriate for the dinner. Also this is nice to serve for home visitors.

It is good to remind that mac n cheese is full of fat and carbohiderate. Therefore doing physical exercises or engaging in physical activities in order to burn excess energy is essencial.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounce of Macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of Butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Salt
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of Milk
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large bowl and pour it with lightly salted water. Put it on heat and add Macaroni to it when it comes to boil. Stir occasionally and cook for about 8 minutes. Take this off from heat and drain the water.
  2. In the same time put a pan on heat and add butter into it. Stir continuesly.
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper in to the melted butter and stir until smooth for about five minutes.
  4. Add milk little by little into the mixture and stir continuesly. Don't let milk burn.
  5. Add Chedar cheese into the mixture and stir until melted for 2 to 4 minutes
  6. Add the macaroni into this cheese souce and mix well until coated
  7. Serve hot and enjoy
